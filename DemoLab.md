DemoLab is the project I use to prototype rendering techniques.

## Features

* Mixed renderer : deferred renderer + forward pass for transparent geometry
* PBR workflow
* Shadow mapping
* Light bloom
* SSAO
* ImGui based interface
* Procedural snow material
* Animated water surface
